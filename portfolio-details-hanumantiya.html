Details of Hanumantiya

Hanumantiya Tapu is one of the most popular tourist destinations of Khandwa for its water activities. Recently, the place has been developed as a great sightseeing adventure by the Madhya Pradesh Tourism Development Centre. Being located at a distance of 47 kilometers from the mainland of Khandwa, reaching Hanumantiya Tapu is now more accessible with well-developed transportation routes.

Locals of the nearby villages and locations plan their weekends here to enjoy the Indira Sagar Damâ€™s retreating backwaters. This island surprises you with its cruising, Floating Fantasy, trekking, bird watching, and botanical trail adventures. A simple stroll through the surrounding region will introduce you with the wildlife that thrives in here. Peacocks, black storks, a small cormorant, and European Ostray are pure delights for all the avid bird observers.

Loaded with heaps of undulating terrains, the freshwater island of Hanumantiya Tapu sends an open invitation to all the trekking freaks. The twists and turns of these undulating terrains make it a perfect spot for all cycling enthusiasts to challenge their skills here.
