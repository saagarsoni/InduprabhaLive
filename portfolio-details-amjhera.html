Details of Amjhera

Amjhera village is in the south-east corner of Sardarpur Tahsil is about 23 km from Sardarpur and about 27 km to the north-west of Dhar.
Amka Jhamka Temple is the most famous temple of village. Temple is dedicated to Goddess Amka Jhamka.
The temple is said to be of the period of Pandavas of Mahabharat.
The fort of Amjhera built of stones and bricks was constructed by Raja Ram Singh Rathore of Jodhpur in the 18-19th Century A.D.
The Raja boldly revolted against British rule whose officers caught and hanged him to death at Indore during the days of the great revolt of 1857.
